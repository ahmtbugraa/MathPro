### 1. "math"
- **Frequency:** 64% of competitors
- **Implementation:** MUST appear in app title (first 15 chars)
- **Priority:** CRITICAL -- non-negotiable for discoverability
- **Estimated search volume:** Very High (100K+/month)

### 2. "solver" / "solve"
- **Frequency:** 43%/11% of competitors
- **Implementation:** Title or subtitle
- **Priority:** CRITICAL
- **Estimated search volume:** High (50K+/month for "math solver")

### 3. "AI"
- **Frequency:** 68% of competitors
- **Implementation:** Title or subtitle
- **Priority:** HIGH (expected by users in 2026, but becoming noise)
- **Note:** Consider whether to lead with AI or treat it as supporting keyword

### 4. "step-by-step" / "step by step"
- **Frequency:** 0% of competitors -- NOBODY uses this
- **Implementation:** Subtitle (ideal differentiator)
- **Priority:** HIGH OPPORTUNITY
- **Estimated search volume:** High ("step by step math solver" is common search)
- **Why:** This is the #1 uncontested high-intent keyword

### 5. "homework"
- **Frequency:** 32% of competitors
- **Implementation:** Subtitle or keyword field
- **Priority:** HIGH
- **Estimated search volume:** High (students search "homework help")

### 6. "problem"
- **Frequency:** 18% of competitors
- **Implementation:** Subtitle (as "Problem Solver")
- **Priority:** HIGH -- "math problem solver" is a major search phrase
