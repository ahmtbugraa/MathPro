## KEY FINDINGS SUMMARY

### Market Facts:
- **Category leader:** Gauth (1.36M ratings, formerly Gauthmath)
- **Highest rated:** Mathway (4.88 rating)
- **Discontinued:** Socratic by Google + Microsoft Math Solver (opportunity!)
- **Minimum viable rating:** 4.5+ to be competitive
- **Average rating:** 4.65 across top 28 apps

### Biggest ASO Opportunity:
**"Step-by-Step"** -- used by ZERO competitors in their title/subtitle despite being one of the highest-intent search terms for math solver apps. This is the single most valuable keyword gap in the entire category.

### Recommended Name:
**MathPro - AI Math Problem Solver**
**Subtitle: Step-by-Step Homework Helper**

This captures: math (critical), AI (expected), problem solver (high intent), step-by-step (zero competition), homework (student intent), helper (secondary keyword).
