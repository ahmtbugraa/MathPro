# Competitor Intelligence - MathPro (Math Solver Category)
**Data Source:** iTunes Search API (US App Store)
**Date Fetched:** 2026-03-26
**Category:** Education / Math Solver

---

## MARKET OVERVIEW

The math solver category is **dominated by a few giants** with a long tail of smaller competitors. Key finding: **Socratic by Google** and **Microsoft Math Solver** have both been **removed from the App Store**, opening a gap in the "big tech" math solver space.

**Total addressable ratings in top 10:** ~3.3M+ ratings
**Average rating across category:** 4.65+
**Minimum viable rating to compete:** 4.5+

## NAMING PATTERN ANALYSIS

### Most Successful Pattern: "Brand: Keyword Subtitle" (46% of apps)
Examples:
- Gauth: AI Study Companion (1.3M ratings)
- Mathway: Math Problem Solver (425K ratings)
- Symbolab - AI Math Solver App (26K ratings)

### Second Pattern: "Brand - Keyword Subtitle" (36% of apps)
Examples:
- Chegg Study - Homework Help (205K ratings)
- Nerd AI - Math Problem Solver (33K ratings)
- Cymath - Math Problem Solver (15K ratings)

### Brand Name Patterns:
- **Compound words:** Photomath, Mathway, FastMath, SnapCalc, MathPapa, MathMaster, MathAce
- **Real words:** Brainly, Studdy, Solvely, Solvo
- **Acronym/Tech:** Question.AI, Answer.AI, Quiz AI, Nerd AI
- **Abstract:** Gauth, Symbolab, Cymath

## COMPETITIVE POSITIONING RECOMMENDATIONS

### What ALL top competitors do well:
1. Free to download (100% freemium model)
2. High ratings (4.5+ minimum to compete)
3. Education + one secondary category (Productivity or Utilities)
4. Short, memorable brand names (1-2 syllables ideal)

### What competitors do poorly:
1. Most waste keyword space -- single-word brands with no subtitle keywords
2. Nobody uses "step-by-step" despite it being the #1 user intent
3. No competitor emphasizes "offline" capability
4. No competitor highlights Apple Pencil / handwriting input
5. "AI" is overused (64% of apps) -- becoming noise rather than differentiator
6. No competitor focuses on practice/learning beyond solving

### MathPro Competitive Advantages to Emphasize:
1. **Step-by-step solutions** in the title (nobody does this)
2. **Camera + Apple Pencil** dual input (unique)
3. **Practice problems** after solving (learning, not just answers)
4. **Offline basic solving** (no competitor mentions)
5. **Clean, green branding** (all competitors use blue/purple)
