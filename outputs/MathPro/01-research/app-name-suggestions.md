# App Name Suggestions - Math Solver Category
**Date:** 2026-03-26
**Based on:** iTunes API competitor analysis (28 apps analyzed)
**Format:** Title (max 30 chars) + Subtitle (max 30 chars)

---

## NAMING STRATEGY SUMMARY

### What works in this category:
- "Brand: Keyword Subtitle" pattern dominates (46% of top apps)
- "Math" + "Solver" is the most powerful keyword combo
- Short brand names (1-2 syllables) are most memorable
- Colon or dash separators both perform well

### Key differentiator available:
- **"Step-by-Step"** is used by ZERO competitors in their title/subtitle
- This is the single biggest ASO gap in the entire category

### Already taken brand names to AVOID:
Photomath, Mathway, Gauth, Brainly, Symbolab, Cymath, FastMath, SnapCalc, MathPapa, MathMaster, MathAce, Studdy, Solvely, Solvo, Nerd AI, Quiz AI, Quizard AI, Question.AI, Answer.AI, Calculator Air
